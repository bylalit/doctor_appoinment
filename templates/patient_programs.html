Patient Programs

Empowering patients through support, care, and education

Alivius

An integrated digital cancer care platform designed to support patients and healthcare professionals throughout the treatment journey. It combines remote monitoring, treatment management, and mental health support into a seamless experience, helping patients track their health, manage side effects, and access personalized resources. With clinically validated tools and real-time alerts, Alivius enhances care coordination, empowers informed decision-making, and improves overall quality of life for cancer patients across multiple geographies.

Joint Health Revolution

The program supports people living with osteoarthritis by helping them understand how to care for their joints. These doctor-led sessions help patients learn about the right exercises, healthy habits, and ways to manage pain, and stay active.

Radar Nasofaringe

An awareness initiative focused on nasopharyngeal cancer (NPC)—a rare and often overlooked disease. Through partnerships with advocacy groups like Oncoguia, the program promotes early diagnosis, patient education, and prepares the ground for future treatment options like Toripalimab.

Awareness For Life - Lifestyle Diseases Awareness Program

Launched in 2010, Dr. Reddy’s Foundation for Health Education’s Awareness for Life program, is one of India’s largest preventive health initiatives led by medical doctors - delivering over 17,000 sessions across 450+ cities to empower communities with health knowledge. From corporates to schools, housing societies, and old age homes, it has reached over 700,000 people with timely, relevant health education.

BetaCare

A patient assistance initiative offering both medical and emotional support. Aims to enhance treatment adherence and health outcomes. Delivers tailored educational materials to empower patients. Encourages proactive involvement in managing their condition.

Vega Club - Education program for IBD patients

Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD) is a chronic medical condition, which if left untreated, may manifest into more severe conditions. Understanding IBD may help patients to be less anxious about the condition, which may ease the severity of symptoms. Once diagnosed, patients should be on therapy to control and suppress symptoms, and ensure symptom free days. Adherence, thus, is extremely important. Vega Club is a patient support initiative in which gastroenterologists educate patients on adherence and disease management.

Mintop Daily

Mintop Daily is a digital platform that supports people with hair loss and thinning hair. Backed by dermatology and the trusted Mintop products, it offers expert advice, product tips, and real success stories to guide users on their hair regrowth journey. Whether trying Minoxidil or looking for lifestyle tips, Mintop Daily helps individuals work toward healthier, fuller hair.

Sankalp

A patient support initiative designed to ensure newly diagnosed multiple myeloma (MM) patients get the right start with a comprehensive care approach. It offers a starter kit in the first month, essential diagnostic tests, side-effect management resources, and telecounselling support. Complementing this, Dr. Reddy’s also contributes to early breast cancer detection through Thermalytix, an innovative AI-powered screening technology that enhances accessibility and accuracy in diagnosis.
